Santa Monica Beach To Reopen This Weekend
Fun In The Sun, For Some
For many Californians, life is getting back to normal as the shelter-in-place order have been lifted. As the pier begins to bustle, the Albright ... reopens it's indoor and outdoor dining areas. This rustic-chic re-do of a veteran fish house withlocal fare, craft beer and communal tables is the perfect spot to reunite with friends.
Treats, Eats, On The Beach
The pier is home to many treats, eats, and activities. From face-painting to fishing, whether you're bird watching or enjoying live performances,... you can count on the local restaurants to fuel your adventures.
